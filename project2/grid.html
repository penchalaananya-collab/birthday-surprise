Popular Cities

  • Hyderabad
  • Chennai
  • Bangalore

Hyderabad

Hyderabad is the capital of Telangana and is famous for Charminar and rich history. It is also a major IT hub in India.

The city is known for Hyderabadi Biryani, pearls, and a blend of modern and traditional culture.

Chennai

Chennai is the capital of Tamil Nadu and lies on the eastern coast of India. It is famous for Marina Beach and temples.

The city is a cultural and educational hub and plays a major role in automobile industries.

Bangalore

Bangalore is the capital of Karnataka and is known as the Silicon Valley of India.

The city has a pleasant climate, IT parks, startups, and vibrant nightlife.
